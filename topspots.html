TOP TOURIST SPOTS TO VISIT

BANFF NATIONAL PARK

Nestled in the Canadian Rockies, Banff National Park offers jaw-dropping mountain scenery, crystal-clear lakes, and charming towns. It's perfect for hiking, skiing, and enjoying the outdoors.

CN Tower

The CN Tower is an iconic symbol of Toronto and Canada. Once the tallest free-standing structure in the world, it offers amazing city views and even a thrilling glass floor experience.

Niagara Falls

One of the most famous waterfalls in the world, Niagara Falls is a breathtaking natural wonder located on the border between Canada and the USA. It's known for its powerful flow and stunning views.
